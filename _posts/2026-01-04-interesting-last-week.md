The Chinese &quot;Wanzi Brocade Pattern&quot; is a traditional decorative motif formed by the continuous repetition of the &quot;卍&quot; (pronounced &quot;wan&quot;) character.  It symbolizes continuity, longevity, and abundant blessings.
